## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Wah ka Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/24-02-01.svg)

Wah ka Ra

wꜢḥ-kꜢ-rꜤ

The enduring one belonging to the ka of Ra

ra-wAH-kA

Mariette, _Le Serapeum de Memphis_ (1857), pl. 34

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 202-203, 1:T
