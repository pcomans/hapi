## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Djed ankh Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/16-13-01.svg)

Djed ankh Ra

ḏd-Ꜥnḫ-rꜤ

The firm one belonging to the life of Ra

ra-Dd-anx

Daressy, _Notes et remarques_, RecTrav **20** (1898): 72

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 102-103, b:T1
