## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Men kheper Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/36-02-04.svg)

Men kheper Ra

mn-ḫpr-rꜤ

The established one of the manifestation of Ra

ra:mn-xpr

The poetic stele, Louvre C 100

Petrie, _A history of Egypt_, III, 293 (fig. 121)

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 196-197, 8:T
