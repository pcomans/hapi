## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Sekha en Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-70-01.svg)

Sekha en Ra

sḫꜤ.n-rꜤ

The one whom Ra has caused to appear

ra-s-xa:1-a:Y1:n

Naville, _The XIth dynasty temple at Deir el-Bahari_, II, plate X e

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 100-101, 44:T
