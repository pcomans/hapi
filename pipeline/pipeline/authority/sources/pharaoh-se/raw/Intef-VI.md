# Intef VI in hieroglyphs

The fifth pharaoh of the [Seventeenth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/17), _a.k.a. Nubkheperra Intef_

| Precedessor<br>[Intef V](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Intef-V)<br>Successor<br>[Intef VII](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Intef-VII) |
| Reign of **Intef VI** |
| Ryholt | 1571–1566 |

Brother to Intef V, ruled Upper Egypt from Thebes during the late Second Intermediate Period, maintaining peaceful relations with the Fifteenth Dynasty, likely paying tribute to the dynasty in the north.

## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.
