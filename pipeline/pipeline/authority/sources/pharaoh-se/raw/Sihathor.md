## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Men wadj Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-27-01.svg)

Men wadj Ra

mn-wꜢḏ-rꜤ

The established and flourishing one of Ra

ra:mn-wAD

Revue d'Egyptologie, 28, 70; 35, 196

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 96-97, 23:T
