## The Royal Titulary

From the hieroglyphic records

## Nebty name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nebty.svg)

The “Two Ladies”, the _goddesses Nekhbet and Wadjet_, representing Upper and Lower Egypt respectively.

Nebty name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/15-03-01.svg)

Waf pedjut

wꜤf-pḏwt

The one who has subdued the Nine Bows

W-pD:pD:pD

Doorjamb, Cairo TD-8316

_Pharaonen und Fremde - Dynastien im Dunkel_, 150-152 \[126\]

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 116-117, d:N

## Golden Horus name

![Golden Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_gold.svg)

Horus perched on Gold, which was associated with eternity, but its meaning is disputed.

Golden Horus name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/15-03-02.svg)

Ir ta shef

ꞽr-tꜢš.f

The one who has made his (own) border

ir-t:U30-A-N37:Z9:f

Doorjamb, Cairo TD-8316

_Pharaonen und Fremde - Dynastien im Dunkel_, 150-152 \[126\]

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 116-117, d:G
