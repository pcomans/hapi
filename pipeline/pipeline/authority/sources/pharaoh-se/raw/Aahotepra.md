## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Aa hotep Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/14-04-01.svg)

Aa hotep Ra

ꜤꜢ-ḥtp-rꜤ

The great one is the (very) peace of Ra

ra:aA-a:Htp

Newberry, _Scarabs_, pl. 22, No. 2-3

Ryholt, _The Political Situation in Egypt during the Second Intermediate Period_ (1997) , 47, 364-366

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 118-119, p:T
