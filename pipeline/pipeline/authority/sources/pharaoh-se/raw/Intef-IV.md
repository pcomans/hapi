## The Royal Titulary

From the hieroglyphic records

## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Sehotep ka Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-23-01.svg)

Sehotep ka Ra

sḥtp-kꜢ-rꜤ

The one who has gained the favor of the ka of Ra

ra-s-Htp:t\*p-kA

Lower part of statue (Cairo Museum JE 67834)

Donadoni, _Nuovo testi di Medinet Madi_, 6

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 94-95, 18:T1
