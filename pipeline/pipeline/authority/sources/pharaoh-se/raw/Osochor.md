Osorkon, an ancient Meshwesh Libyan name, was Hellenised as Osochor in Manetho. His reign is completely unknown, except to herald the coming of the Libyan Twenty-second Dynasty. He is often referred to as _Osorkon the Elder_ to distinguish him from the four Osorkons of the next dynasty, who had long since been assigned Roman numerals.
