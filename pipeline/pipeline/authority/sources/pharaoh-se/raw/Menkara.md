# Menkara in hieroglyphs

The second pharaoh of the [Eighth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/8), _a.k.a. Menkare_

| Precedessor<br>[Netjerikara](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Netjerikara)<br>Successor<br>[Neferkara II](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Neferkara-II) |
| Reign of **Menkara** |

Menkara's name is only securely attested on Seti I's Abydos king list (number 41,) and the Turin papyrus unfortunately has a notation of a lacuna where his name would have been found. The tomb of queen Neit in at Saqqara has the queen in front of a damaged cartouche that reads _..kara_, with enough space to have helf the Men sign.

## Throne name

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.
