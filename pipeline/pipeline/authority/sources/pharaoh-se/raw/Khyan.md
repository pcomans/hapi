# Khyan in hieroglyphs

The third pharaoh of the [Fifteenth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/15), _a.k.a. Khian, Khayan_

| Precedessor<br>[Apachnas](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Apachnas)<br>Successor<br>[Apepi](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Apepi) |
| Reign of **Khyan** |
| Ryholt | 1621–1581 |

Africanus' _Staan_ was likely an error copying the name _Siaan_. Josephus' names _Iannas_ in the corresponding place, which would seem to be a mistake for Khyan's eldest son Yanassi.

## Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_horus.svg)

The oldest form of the name of a pharaoh, a falcon representing Horus is perched on the palace facade.

Horus name

Lower part of usurped statue, (Cairo Museum, CG 389, JE 28574)

## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Seuser en Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/15-04-02.svg)

Seuser en Ra

ra-s\*F78:n

Lower part of usurped statue, (Cairo Museum, CG 389, JE 28574)

Naville, _Bubastis_, 23, pls. 21; 35A

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 114-115, 4:T
