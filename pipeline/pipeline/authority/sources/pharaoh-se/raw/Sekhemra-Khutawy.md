## Nebty name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nebty.svg)

The “Two Ladies”, the _goddesses Nekhbet and Wadjet_, representing Upper and Lower Egypt respectively.

Nebty name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-16-02.svg)

wḥm-ḏd

## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Sekhem Ra khu tawy](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-16-04.svg)

Sekhem Ra khu tawy

sḫm-rꜤ ḫwi-tꜢwi

The powerful one of Ra is the protection of the Two Lands

ra-sxm-x-D43:N17:N17

Naville, _Bubastis_, 15, plate XXXIII (i) architrave now in British Museum EA1100

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 88-89, 3:T1
