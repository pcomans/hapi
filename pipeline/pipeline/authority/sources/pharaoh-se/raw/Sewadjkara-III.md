## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Sewadj ka Ra*](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/14-11-01.svg)

Sewadj ka Ra\*

s-wꜢḏ-kꜤ-rꜤ

The one whom the ka of Ra has made thrive

ra-s-wAD-kA-1

Kitchen, _Ramesside Inscriptions_ , II, 838:10

Turin King List 9.6

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 108-109. 7:T
