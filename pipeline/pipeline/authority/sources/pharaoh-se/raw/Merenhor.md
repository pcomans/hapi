# Merenhor in hieroglyphs

The seventh pharaoh of the [Eighth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/8),

| Precedessor<br>[Neferkara Khendu](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Neferkara-Khendu)<br>Successor<br>[Neferkamin I](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Neferkamin-I) |
| Reign of **Merenhor** |

Merenhor is found on the Abydos king list number 46, while the Turin papyrus has a notation of a lacuna where the name would have been found.

## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.
