# Neferirkara II in hieroglyphs

A pharaoh(?) of the [Eighth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/8), _a.k.a. Neferirkare II_

| Precedessor<br>[Neferkauhor](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Neferkauhor)<br>Successor<br>[Meribra Khety I](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Meribra-Khety-I) |

Neferirkara (the roman numeral II is used to differentiate him from his namesake, Neferirkara Kakai) was probably the last king before Herkleopolitan Dynasty IX overthrew the already weak regime.
