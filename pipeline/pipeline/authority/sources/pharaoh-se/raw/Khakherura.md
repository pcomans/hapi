## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Kha kheru Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/14-07-01.svg)

Kha kheru Ra

ḫꜤ-ḫrw-rꜤ

The (very) appearance of the voice of Ra

ra-xa:a\*O-xrw-y

Scribe probably misread the sign P8 for the similar U33. **Corrected sign shown**. See variant for original.
