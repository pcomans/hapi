## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Wah ib Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-31-01.svg)

Wah ib Ra

wꜢḥ-ꞽb-rꜤ

The enduring one is the (very) mind of Ra

ra-wAH-ib

Petrie, _Historical scarabs_, no. 323

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 96-97, 26:T1
