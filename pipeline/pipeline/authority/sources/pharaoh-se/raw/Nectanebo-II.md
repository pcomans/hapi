## Nebty names

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nebty.svg)

The “Two Ladies”, the _goddesses Nekhbet and Wadjet_, representing Upper and Lower Egypt respectively.

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/30-03-04.svg)

shrw-ꞽb-nṯrw

The one who has pleased the heart of Gods

s-h:r-ib:1-nTrw

## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Senedjem ib Ra, <i>setep en Amun</i>](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/30-03-10.svg)

Senedjem ib Ra, _setep en Amun_

snḏm-ꞽb-rꜤ stp.n-ꞽmn

Pleasing to the Heart is Ra, chosen of Amun

Lefebvre, _Égypte gréco-romaine, IV_, ASAE 13 (1914): 222
