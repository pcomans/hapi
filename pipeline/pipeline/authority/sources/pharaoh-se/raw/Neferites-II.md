# Neferites II in hieroglyphs

The fourth pharaoh of the [Twenty-ninth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/29), _a.k.a. Nepherites II, Nefaarud II_

| Precedessor<br>[Psammuthis](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Psammuthis)<br>Successor<br>[Muthis](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Muthis) |
| Reign of **Neferites II** |
|  | 380 |
| Dodson | 380 |
| Manetho | 4 months |

Only ruled for 4 months before being killed, most likely on orders of Nectanebo I. Due to his short reign, his name does not appear on any monuments. His name is only known from Manetho and the Demotic Chronicle\*.
