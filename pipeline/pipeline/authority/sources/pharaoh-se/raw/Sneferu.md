# Sneferu in hieroglyphs

The first pharaoh of the [Fourth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/4), _a.k.a. Snefru, Snofru_

| Precedessor<br>[Huni](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Huni)<br>Successor<br>[Khufu](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Khufu) |
| Reign of **Sneferu** |
| AE Chronology | 2543–2510 |
| von Beckerath | 2614–2579 |
| Shaw | 2613–2589 |
| Dodson | 2520–2470 |
| Allen | 2575–2551 |
| Arnold | 2600–2555 |
| Malek | 2573–2549 |
| Redford | 2649–2609 |
| Turin King List | 24 years |
| Manetho (Africanus) | 29 years |

According to Manetho, Sneferu ruled for 29 years, although the Turin King-list only lists 24. A biennial cattle-count would indicate a 48 year reign, but it is likely that the counts sometimes happened yearly, but he probably ruled 30 to 40 years, which would explain how he managed to build three pyramids. It is known that he ruled for at least three years after the 24th cattle-count.
