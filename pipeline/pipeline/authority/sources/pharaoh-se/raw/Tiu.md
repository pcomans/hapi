# Tiu in hieroglyphs

The third pharaoh of the [Predynastic kings](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/predynastic), _a.k.a. Teyew_

| Precedessor<br>[Khayu](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Khayu)<br>Successor<br>[Tjesh](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Tjesh) |
| Reign of **Tiu** |
|  | 4th millenium BCE |

**Tiu** is found on the Palermo Stone as ruler of a predynastic Lower Egypt. As this is the only known record of the name, it is impossible to know whether he was a real or mythical.
