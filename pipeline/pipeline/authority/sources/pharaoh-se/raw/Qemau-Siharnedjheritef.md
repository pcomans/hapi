## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Hotep ib Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-06-01.svg)

Hotep ib Ra

ḥtp-ꞽb-rꜤ

The one who has gained the favor of the heart of Ra

ra-Htp:t\*p-ib

ASAE , 52, plate IX

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 90-91, 9:T1

Throne name variant

![Sehotep ib Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-06-02.svg)

Sehotep ib Ra

s-ḥtp-ꞽb-rꜤ

The one who has gained the favor of the heart of Ra

ra-s-Htp:t\*p-ib-Z1
