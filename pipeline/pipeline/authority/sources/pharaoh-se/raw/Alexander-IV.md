## Horus names

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_horus.svg)

The oldest form of the name of a pharaoh, a falcon representing Horus is perched on the palace facade.

Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/32-03-01.svg)

Hunu user pehty

ḥwnw wsr-pḥtꞽ

The youthful one, powerful of strength

H-wn:n-A17-wsr-F9:F9

Edgar, _Notes from the Delta_, ASAE 11 (1911): 92

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 232-233, 3:H1

## Nebty name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nebty.svg)

The “Two Ladies”, the _goddesses Nekhbet and Wadjet_, representing Upper and Lower Egypt respectively.

Nebty name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/32-03-03.svg)

Mery netjeru redji enef iaut en itef

mrꞽ-nṯrw rḏ-n.f-ꞽꜢwt-n-ꞽt.f

Beloved of the gods, to whom the office of his father was given

nTrw:N36-r:a:n:f-O44-t:Z1-n:t:f:f

Mariette, _Monuments Divers_, II, plate 14

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 232-233, 3:N

## Golden Horus name

![Golden Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_gold.svg)

Horus perched on Gold, which was associated with eternity, but its meaning is disputed.

Golden Horus name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/32-03-04.svg)

Heqa nakht em ta er djeref

ḥḳꜢ-nḫt-m-tꜢ-r-ḏr.f

Victorious ruler in the entire land

HqA-q:D40:Aa15-tA:M36:f

Mariette, _Monuments Divers_, II, plate 14

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 232-233, 3:G

## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Haa ib Ra, <i>setep en Amun</i>](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/32-03-05.svg)

Haa ib Ra, _setep en Amun_

ḥꜤꜤ-ꞽb-rꜤ stp.n-imn

Who (continually) rejoices over the mind of Ra, chosen by Amun

C12A\\-C2A-H-a:a-stp:n-ib:1

Mariette, _Monuments Divers_, II, plate 14

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 232-233, 3:T2
