# Neferkara II in hieroglyphs

The third pharaoh of the [Eighth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/8), _a.k.a. Neferkare II_

| Precedessor<br>[Menkara](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Menkara)<br>Successor<br>[Neferkara Neby](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Neferkara-Neby) |
| Reign of **Neferkara II** |

Neferkara II is only known from the Abydos king list number 42, while the Turin papyrus unfortunately has a notation of a lacuna where the name would have been found.

## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.
