## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Hedj kheper Ra, <i>setep en Ra</i>](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/22-05-01.svg)

Hedj kheper Ra, _setep en Ra_

ḥḏ-ḫpr-rꜤ stp-n-rꜤ

The radiant one is the (very) manifestation or Ra, chosen by Ra

ra-S1-xpr-stp&n&ra

Posener, Malinine, Vercoutter, _Catalogue des steles du Serapeum de Memphis_, no. 19

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 186-187, 3:T
