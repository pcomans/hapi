The ninth pharaoh of the [Fifth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/5), _a.k.a. Wenis, Unis_

Unas is present on the Abydos (33) and Saqqara (23) king lists, as well as in the Turin papyrus (4.25). He is also included on Manetho's king list as the last ruler of the fifth dynasty, _Onnos_.

The oldest form of the name of a pharaoh, a falcon representing Horus is perched on the palace facade.

## Nebty name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nebty.svg)
