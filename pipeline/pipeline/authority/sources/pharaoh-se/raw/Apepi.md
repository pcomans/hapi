# Apepi in hieroglyphs

The fourth pharaoh of the [Fifteenth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/15), _a.k.a. Nebkhepeshre, Aqenenre, Aweserre, Apophis_

| Precedessor<br>[Khyan](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Khyan)<br>Successor<br>[Khamudy](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Khamudy) |
| Reign of **Apepi** |
| AE Chronology | 1575–1540 |
| Ryholt | 1581–1541 |
| Shaw | c. 1555 |
| Dodson | 1585–1545 |

It was once believed that there were two separate kings named Apepi, but it is now known that there was only one. For unknown reasons, he changed his name twice during his reign.

## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.
