# Mekh in hieroglyphs

The seventh pharaoh of the [Predynastic kings](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/predynastic), _a.k.a. Imykhet_

| Precedessor<br>[Wenegbu](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Wenegbu)<br>Successor<br>[Narmer](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Narmer) |
| Reign of **Mekh** |
|  | 4th millenium BCE |

**Mekh** is found on the Palermo Stone as ruler of a predynastic Lower Egypt. As this is the only known record of the name, it is impossible to know whether he was a real or mythical.
