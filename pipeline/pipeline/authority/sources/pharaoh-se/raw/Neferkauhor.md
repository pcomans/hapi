# Neferkauhor in hieroglyphs

The sixteenth pharaoh of the [Eighth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/8),

| Precedessor<br>[Neferkaura](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Neferkaura)<br>Successor<br>[Neferirkara II](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Neferirkara-II) |
| Reign of **Neferkauhor** |
| AE Chronology | 2122–2120 |
| Turin King List | 2y 1m 1d |

Neferkauhor's throne name is found on the Abydos king list, and in the Turin Canon his name is lost in lacuna recording only a reign of 2 years, 1 month and 1 day. His Horus name is present on fragmentary decrees found at Coptos.

## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.
