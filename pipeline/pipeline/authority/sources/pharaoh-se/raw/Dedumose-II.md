## The Royal Titulary

From the hieroglyphic records

## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Djed nefer Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/16-12-01.svg)

Djed nefer Ra

ḏd-nfr-rꜤ

The stable one of the perfection of Ra

ra-Dd-nfr

Gebelein stela, (Cairo Museum, CG 20533, JE 29239)

Daressy, _Notes et remarques_, RecTrav **14** (1893): 26

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 100-101, 37:T2

## Birth name

![Nomen or birth name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_sara.svg)

Personal name given at birth. Also known as the _Nomen_.

Birth name

![Dedumose](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/16-12-02.svg)

Dedumose

ḏd(w)-msꞽ(w)

The one (whose) birth has been granted

D37:D37-w-ms-s

Gebelein stela, (Cairo Museum, CG 20533, JE 29239)

Daressy, _Notes et remarques_, RecTrav **14** (1893): 26

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 100-101, 37:E2
