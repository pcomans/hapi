# Amenemope in hieroglyphs

The fourth pharaoh of the [Twenty-first Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/21),

| Precedessor<br>[Psusennes I](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Psusennes-I)<br>Successor<br>[Osochor](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Osochor) |
| Reign of **Amenemope** |
| AE Chronology | 1002–993 |
| von Beckerath | 996–985 |
| Shaw | 993–984 |
| Dodson | 984–974 |
| Manetho | 9 years |

Amenemope succeeded the long reign of his father after a period of coregency. Very little is known about his reign.

## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.
