## Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_horus.svg)

The oldest form of the name of a pharaoh, a falcon representing Horus is perched on the palace facade.

Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-15-01.svg)

Hotep ib tawy

ḥtp-ꞽb-tꜢwꞽ

The mind of the Two Lands is satisfied

Htp:ib-N17:N17

deMorgan, _Fouilles a Dahchour: Mars - Juin 1894_, 93, fig. 214

Wooden naos, CG 70035

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 92-93, 13:H

## Nebty name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nebty.svg)

The “Two Ladies”, the _goddesses Nekhbet and Wadjet_, representing Upper and Lower Egypt respectively.

Nebty name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-15-02.svg)

Nefer khau

nfr-ḫꜤw

Perfect of appearances

nfr-xa:a-w-Y1:Z2

deMorgan, _Fouilles a Dahchour: Mars - Juin 1894_, 93, fig. 214

Wooden naos, CG 70035

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 92-93, 13:N

![Golden Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_gold.svg)

## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Aut ib Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-15-04.svg)

Aut ib Ra

Ꜣwt-ꞽb-rꜤ

The (very) joy of Ra

ra-F40-ib

deMorgan, _Fouilles a Dahchour: Mars - Juin 1894_, 93, fig. 214

Wooden naos, CG 70035

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 92-93, 13:T1

Throne name variant

![Aut ib Ra*](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-15-05.svg)

Aut ib Ra\*

Ꜣwt-ꞽb-rꜤ
