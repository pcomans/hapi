# Hudjefa I in hieroglyphs

The tenth pharaoh of the [Second Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/2),

| Precedessor<br>[Neferkasokar](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Neferkasokar)<br>Successor<br>[Khasekhemwy](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Khasekhemwy) |
| Reign of **Hudjefa I** |
| von Beckerath | 2711–2709 |
| Turin King List | 11y 8m 4d |
| Manetho (Africanus) | 48 years |

The name _Hudjefa_ appears in the Saqqara and Turin king lists. Both describe Hudjefa as the immediate successor of Neferkasokar and the predecessor of Khasekhemwy. Hudjefa is not a proper name, but instead describes that the original name was unreadable to the scribe compiling the list.
