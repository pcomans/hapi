Pepi I pursued an expansion of the kingdom into Nubia, and traded with far away places. Pepi's pyramid complex, **_Men-nefer_ Pepi**,, later gave name to the whole city of Memphis. The name means _"enduring and perfect (is the pyramid) of Pepi"_.

His first throne name, Nefersahor, _perfect is the protection of Horus_, was later altered to Meryra, _beloved of Ra_, perhaps in response to the increased influence of the sun cult of Ra.
