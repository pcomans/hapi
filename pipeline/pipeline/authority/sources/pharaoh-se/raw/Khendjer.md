# Khendjer in hieroglyphs

A pharaoh(?) of the [Thirteenth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/13),

Khendjer is the earliest known Semitic king of a native Egyptian dynasty and his name has been interpreted as the foreign name _Hnzr_ and equated with the Semitic personal name for "boar". His name is present in the Turin Canon, and only a few other finds contain his name. He had a small pyramid built at Saqqara.
