# Peribsen in hieroglyphs

The seventh pharaoh of the [Second Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/2), _a.k.a. Seth-Peribsen, Perabsen_

| Precedessor<br>[Sekhemib](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Sekhemib)<br>Successor<br>[Sneferka](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Sneferka) |
| Reign of **Peribsen** |
| Redford | 2660–2650 |

Instead of the traditional falcon perched on the serekh of the Horus name, Peribsen chose instead the Seth-animal, likely due to religious shifts. His name is missing from the New Kingdom king lists, but the reason for this is unknown.

## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)
