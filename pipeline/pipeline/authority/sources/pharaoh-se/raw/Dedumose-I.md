## Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_horus.svg)

The oldest form of the name of a pharaoh, a falcon representing Horus is perched on the palace facade.

Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/16-11-01.svg)

Wadj khau

wꜢḏ-ḫꜤw

Flourishing of appearances

wAD-xa:a

Stela of the kings son, (Cairo Museum, JE 38917)

Barsanti, _Stèle inédite au nom du roi Radadaouhotep Doudoumes_, ASAE **9** (1909): pl. 1-2

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 100-101, 37:H

## Nebty name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nebty.svg)

The “Two Ladies”, the _goddesses Nekhbet and Wadjet_, representing Upper and Lower Egypt respectively.

Nebty name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/16-11-02.svg)

Shed tawy

šdꞽ-tꜢwꞽ

The one who has rescued the Two Lands

F30:d-N16:N16

Stela of the kings son, (Cairo Museum, JE 38917)

Barsanti, _Stèle inédite au nom du roi Radadaouhotep Doudoumes_, ASAE **9** (1909): pl. 1-2

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 100-101, 37:N

## Golden Horus name

![Golden Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_gold.svg)

Horus perched on Gold, which was associated with eternity, but its meaning is disputed.

Golden Horus name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/16-11-03.svg)

In hotep

ꞽn-ḥtp

The one who has brought peace

ini-Htp:t\*p

Stela of the kings son, (Cairo Museum, JE 38917)

Barsanti, _Stèle inédite au nom du roi Radadaouhotep Doudoumes_, ASAE **9** (1909): pl. 1-2

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 100-101, 37:G

## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Djed hotep Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/16-11-04.svg)

Djed hotep Ra

ḏd-ḥtp-rꜤ

The stable one of the peace of Ra

ra-Dd-Dd-Htp:t\*p

Stela of the kings son, (Cairo Museum, JE 38917)

Barsanti, _Stèle inédite au nom du roi Radadaouhotep Doudoumes_, ASAE **9** (1909): pl. 1-2

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 100-101, 37:T1

## Birth name

![Nomen or birth name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_sara.svg)

Personal name given at birth. Also known as the _Nomen_.

Birth name

![Dedu mesu](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/16-11-05.svg)

Dedu mesu

ḏd(w)-msꞽ(w)

The one (whose) birth has been granted

D37:D37-ms-s-w

Stela of the kings son, (Cairo Museum, JE 38917)

Barsanti, _Stèle inédite au nom du roi Radadaouhotep Doudoumes_, ASAE **9** (1909): pl. 1-2
