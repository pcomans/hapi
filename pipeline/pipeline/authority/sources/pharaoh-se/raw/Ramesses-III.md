# Ramesses III in hieroglyphs

The second pharaoh of the [Twentieth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/20), _a.k.a. Rameses III, Ramses III_

| Precedessor<br>[Setnakht](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Setnakht)<br>Successor<br>[Ramesses IV](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Ramesses-IV) |

| Highest attestation | Year 32 |

The 32 year reign of Ramesses III was marked by political, economic and military problems as foreign invaders flowed into the kingdom. He was murdered in the Harem conspiracy instigated by his second queen, Tiye, who wanted her son Pentawer to succeed Ramesses III instead of the designated prince. The plan failed, and is known to us thanks to the _Judicial Papyrus of Turin_.

He built the great temple of _Medinet Habu_ clearly based on the mortuary temple of Ramesses II, _the Ramesseum_.
