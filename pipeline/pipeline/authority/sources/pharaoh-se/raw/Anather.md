# Anather in hieroglyphs

The first pharaoh of the [Unplaced kings](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/unplaced),

| Precedessor<br>[Inaros](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Inaros)<br>Successor<br>[Harsiese A](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Harsiese-A) |
| Reign of **Anather** |

Anather may have been the first ruler of the Sixteenth Dynasty, or a Canaanite chieftain, possibly contemporary with the Twelfth Dynasty. The scarce evidence for either is inconclusive.
