# Tjesh in hieroglyphs

The fourth pharaoh of the [Predynastic kings](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/predynastic), _a.k.a. Thesh, Tesh_

| Precedessor<br>[Tiu](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Tiu)<br>Successor<br>[Neheb](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Neheb) |
| Reign of **Tjesh** |
|  | 4th millenium BCE |

**Tjesh** is found on the Palermo Stone as ruler of a predynastic Lower Egypt. As this is the only known record of the name, it is impossible to know whether he was a real or mythical.
