## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Semen ka Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-09-01.svg)

Semen ka Ra

smn-kꜢ-rꜤ

The one whom the ka of Ra has established

ra-s-mn:n-kA

Castel & Soukiassian, _Dépôt de stèles dans le sanctuaire du Nouvel Empire au Gebel Zeit_. BIFAO **85**, plate LXII

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 90-91, 8:T1

Throne name variant
