## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Sekhem Ra khu tawy](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/35-03-01.svg)

Sekhem Ra khu tawy

sḫm-rꜤ ḫwꞽ-tꜢwꞽ

The powerful one of Ra is the protection of the Two Lands

ra-sxm-D43:N17:N17

Stela in British Museum, EA 630

Petrie, _Abydos_, II, plate 31 (upper right); 32 (2)

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 88-89, 3:T2
