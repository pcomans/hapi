## The Royal Titulary

From the hieroglyphic records

## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Tut kheper Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/22-04-01.svg)

Tut kheper Ra

twt-ḫpr-rꜤ

The (very) image of the manifestation of Ra

ra-xpr-t&w&t-v/

Bonheme, _Les Chechonqides: Qui, combien?_, Bulletin de la Société française d'Égyptologie **133**, 53-54

## Birth name

![Nomen or birth name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_sara.svg)

Personal name given at birth. Also known as the _Nomen_.

Birth name

![Shoshenq <i>mery Amun</i>](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/22-04-02.svg)

Shoshenq _mery Amun_

ššnḳ mrꞽ-ꞽmn

Shoshenq, beloved of Amun

i-mn:n:N36-M8:M8-n:q

Bonheme, _Les Chechonqides: Qui, combien?_, Bulletin de la Société française d'Égyptologie **133**, 53-54
