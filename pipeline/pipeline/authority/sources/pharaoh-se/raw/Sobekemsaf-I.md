## Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_horus.svg)

The oldest form of the name of a pharaoh, a falcon representing Horus is perched on the palace facade.

Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/17-02-01.svg)

Hetep netjeru

ḥtp-nṯrw

The one with whom the gods are satisfied

Htp-nTrw

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nebty.svg)

## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Sekhem Ra wadj khau](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/17-02-04.svg)

Sekhem Ra wadj khau

sḫm-rꜤ wꜢḏ-ḫꜤw

The powerful one of Ra is flourishing of appearances

ra-sxm-wAD-xa:Z2

Karnak Open Air Museum blocks from Montu temple at Madamud

Lepsius, _Denkmäler aus Ägypten und Äthiopien_, II, 151 K+L

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 124-125, 3:T1
