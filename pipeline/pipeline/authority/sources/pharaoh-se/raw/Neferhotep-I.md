## Horus names

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_horus.svg)

The oldest form of the name of a pharaoh, a falcon representing Horus is perched on the palace facade.

## Nebty name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nebty.svg)

The “Two Ladies”, the _goddesses Nekhbet and Wadjet_, representing Upper and Lower Egypt respectively.

Nebty name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-26-03.svg)

Wep Maat

wp-mꜢꜤt

The one who has inaugurated Maat

F13:p\*Z9-U5:a-t:Y1-H6B

Mariette, _Abydos_, II, plate 28 (1)

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 96-97, 22:N

## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Kha sekhem Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-26-05.svg)

Kha sekhem Ra

ḫꜤꞽ-sḫm-rꜤ

The (very) appearance of the power of Ra

ra-xa:a-Y8

Mariette, _Abydos_, II, plate 28

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 96-97, 22:T

Throne name variant

![Kha sekhem Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-26-06.svg)
