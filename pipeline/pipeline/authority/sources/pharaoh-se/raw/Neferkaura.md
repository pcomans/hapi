# Neferkaura in hieroglyphs

The fifteenth pharaoh of the [Eighth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/8), _a.k.a. Neferkaure_

| Precedessor<br>[Qakara Ibi](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Qakara-Ibi)<br>Successor<br>[Neferkauhor](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Neferkauhor) |
| Reign of **Neferkaura** |
| AE Chronology | 2126–2113 |
| Turin King List | 4y 2m |

Neferkaura's throne name is found on the Abydos king list, and in the Turin Canon his name is missing recording only a reign of 4 year and 2 months.
