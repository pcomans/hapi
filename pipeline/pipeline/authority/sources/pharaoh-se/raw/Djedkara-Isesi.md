Djedkara Isesi is present on all four king lists of the New Kingdom, albeit with different names on each. They are, in chronological order: Karnak (5) has _Issi_, Abydos (32) has _Djedkara_, the Turin papyrus (4.24) has _Djedu_, and Saqqara (28) has _Maatkara_. The discrepancies are likely due to errors by the Ramesside scribes. He is also present on Manetho's king list as _Tankheres_ which is the Greek transcription of Djedkara.
