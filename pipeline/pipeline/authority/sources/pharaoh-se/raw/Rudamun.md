## Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_horus.svg)

The oldest form of the name of a pharaoh, a falcon representing Horus is perched on the palace facade.

Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/23-07-01.svg)

Neb maa kheru

nb-mꜢꜤ-ḫrw

The possessor of righteousness

nb-U4-t-P8

Legrain, _Le temple et les chapelles d'Osiris a Karnak_, RecTrav 22 (1900): 130

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 196-197, 7:H

## Nebty name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nebty.svg)

The “Two Ladies”, the _goddesses Nekhbet and Wadjet_, representing Upper and Lower Egypt respectively.

Nebty name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/23-07-02.svg)

Heken en Maat

ḥkn-n-mꜢꜤt

Who has rejoiced at Maat

H-k:n-m-U1-Aa11:t

Legrain, _Le temple et les chapelles d'Osiris a Karnak_, RecTrav 22 (1900): 130

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 196-197, 7:N

## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![User Maat Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/23-07-03.svg)

User Maat Ra

wsr-mꜢꜤt-rꜤ

The strong one belonging to the Maat of Ra

ra-wsr-U1-Aa11:a:t

Legrain, _Le temple et les chapelles d'Osiris a Karnak_, RecTrav 22 (1900): 130

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 196-197, 7:T1

Throne name variant

![User Maat Ra, <i>setep en Amun</i>](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/23-07-04.svg)

User Maat Ra, _setep en Amun_

wsr-mꜢꜤt-rꜤ stp.n-ꞽmn

The strong one belonging to the Maat of Ra, chosen by Amun

ra-wsr-U2:t-i-mn:n-stp:n

Daressy, _Notes et remarques_, RecTrav **19** (1897): 20

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 196-197, 7:T2
