## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Kheper ka Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/30-01-14.svg)

Kheper ka Ra

ḫpr-kꜢ-rꜤ

The (very) manifestation of the ka of Ra

ra-xpr-kA

Passim

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 226-227, 1:T
