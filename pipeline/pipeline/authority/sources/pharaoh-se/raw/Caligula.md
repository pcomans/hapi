## The Royal Titulary

## Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_horus.svg)

The oldest form of the name of a pharaoh, a falcon representing Horus is perched on the palace facade.

Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/34-03-01.svg)

Ka nakht iakhw setut Ra Iah

kꜢ-nḫt ꞽꜢḫ-stwt-rꜤ-ꞽꜤh

The strong bull, the
light of the sun and the moon's rays

E1-nw\*Z9:D40-G25-x:W-s-t:W-N8-Z2A-C2B-C1A

Lepsius, _Denkmäler aus Ägypten und Äthiopien_, IV, 76e

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 252-253, 3:H

## Kaisaros

The Greek title _Kaisaros_ is the equivalent of the Latin title _Caesar_.

It was always written in a cartouche.

Kaisaros

![Kyseres Kernykes <i>ankh djet</i>](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/34-03-03.svg)

Kyseres Kernykes _ankh djet_

kysrs krwnyḳs?

Caesar Germanicus, living forever

q&Ai-i-s-S56&Ai-i-s-S56:l-z:S56\*r-Aa56-nw-i-i-q:V6-anx-D&t:N17

Lepsius, _Denkmäler aus Ägypten und Äthiopien_, IV, 76e

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 252-253, 3:E1

## Autokrator

The Greek epithet _Autokrator_ is the equivalent of the Latin epithet _Imperator_ (Commander).

It was always written in the cartouche.

Autokrator

![Autokrator heqa heqau mery Ptah Aset](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/34-03-02.svg)

Autokrator heqa heqau mery Ptah Aset

Ꜣwtkrtr ḥḳꜢ-ḥḳꜢw mrꞽ-ptḥ-Ꜣst

Emperor and ruler of rulers, beloved by Ptah and Isis

A23E-HqA-Z2A-A-W\*t:g-rw:d:r-C102\\-C159A

Lepsius, _Denkmäler aus Ägypten und Äthiopien_, IV, 76d

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 252-253, 3:T
