# Merenptah in hieroglyphs

The fourth pharaoh of the [Nineteenth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/19), _a.k.a. Merneptah_

| Precedessor<br>[Ramesses II](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Ramesses-II)<br>Successor<br>[Seti II](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Seti-II) |

| AE Chronology | 1213–1203 |

| Manetho (Africanus) | 20 years |
| Manetho (Eusebius) | 40 years |
| Manetho (Jerome) | 40 years |
| Highest attestation | Year 10 |

Merenptah was the thirteenth son of Ramesses II and ascended the throne when he was at least sixty years old. While Manetho records that he ruled for 20 years, it was probably only about half of that.
