## The Royal Titulary

From the hieroglyphic records

## Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_horus.svg)

The oldest form of the name of a pharaoh, a falcon representing Horus is perched on the palace facade.

Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-10-01.svg)

Sewesekh tawy

swsḫ-tꜢwꞽ

The one who has widened the Two Lands

s-w-s-x:W10-N17:N17

_Mitteilungen des Deutschen Archäologischen Instituts, Abteilung Kairo_ , 36, plate 80a

## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Sehotep ib Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-10-02.svg)

Sehotep ib Ra

sḥtp-ꞽb-rꜤ

The one who has gained the favor of the mind of Ra

ra-s-t-Htp:p-ib

_Mitteilungen des Deutschen Archäologischen Instituts, Abteilung Kairo_ , 36, plate 80 a

Throne name variant

![Hotep ib Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-10-03.svg)

Hotep ib Ra

ra-Htp:t\*p-ib
