# Neferkahor in hieroglyphs

The eleventh pharaoh of the [Eighth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/8),

| Precedessor<br>[Neferkara Tereru](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Neferkara-Tereru)<br>Successor<br>[Neferkara Pepiseneb](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Neferkara-Pepiseneb) |
| Reign of **Neferkahor** |

Neferkahor is found on the Abydos king list number 50, while the Turin papyrus has a notation of a lacuna where the name would have been found.

## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.
