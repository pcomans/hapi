## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Kha ankh Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/13-13-04.svg)

Kha ankh Ra

ḫꜤꞽ-Ꜥnḫ-rꜤ

The living appearance of Ra

ra-xa-anx

Block from Abydos chapel (Louvre C 9)

Red granite pedestal (Leiden Museum van Oudheden AM 109-a)
