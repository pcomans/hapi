## Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_horus.svg)

The oldest form of the name of a pharaoh, a falcon representing Horus is perched on the palace facade.

Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/23-01-01.svg)

The strong bull who appears in Thebes

## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Hedj kheper Ra, <i>setep en Ra</i>](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/23-01-02.svg)

Hedj kheper Ra, _setep en Ra_

ḥḏ-ḫpr-rꜤ stp.n-rꜤ

Radiant is the manifestation of Ra, the chosen one of Ra

ra-S1-xpr-stp&n&ra

The Epigraphic Survey, _The Bubastite portal_, (OIP 74), plate 21 (1)

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 192-193, 1:T1

Throne name variant

![Hedj kheper Ra, <i>setep en Ra, netjer heqa Waset</i>](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/23-01-03.svg)

Hedj kheper Ra, _setep en Ra, netjer heqa Waset_

ḥḏ-ḫpr-rꜤ stp.n-rꜤ nṯr-ḥḳꜢ-wꜢst

Radiant is the manifestation of Ra, the chosen one of Ra, divine ruler of Thebes

ra-S1-xpr-stp&n&ra-nTr-HqA-R19

_Aegyptische Inschriften aus den Königlichen Museen zu Berlin_, II, p. 539 (6964)
