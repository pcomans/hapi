## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![User ib Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/35-01-02.svg)

User ib Ra

wsr-ꞽb-rꜤ

Strong (like) the mind of Ra

ra-wsr-s-r:ib\*1

Tomb CS9 of Senebkay at Abydos

Wegner, "A Royal Necropolis at Abydos", _Near Eastern Archaeology_, 78 (2), 75, Fig. 9

Note: Name not enclosed in a cartouche.
