# Seqenenra in hieroglyphs

The eighth pharaoh of the [Seventeenth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/17), _a.k.a. Seqenenre Tao_

| Precedessor<br>[Senakhtenra](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Senakhtenra)<br>Successor<br>[Kamose](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Kamose) |
| Reign of **Seqenenra** |
| Ryholt | 1558–1554 |
| Dodson | 1558–1554 |
| Franke | ?–1545 |

Seqenenra Tao is credited with starting the liberation of Egypt from the Hyksos fifteenth dynasty in the north. He was however defeated and taken prisoner by the forces of king Apepi and likely executed. His son Kamose continued and expanded the campaign against the Hyksos.

## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.
