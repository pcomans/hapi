# Niuserra Ini in hieroglyphs

The sixth pharaoh of the [Fifth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/5), _a.k.a. Niuserre_

| Turin King List | 11–34 years |
| Manetho (Africanus) | 44 years |

When Neferefra died unexpectedly, his brother Niuserra was set to succeed him, but as he was still only a child, Shepseskara managed to usurp the throne for a year or so, before he in turn was ousted and the rightful king was put on the throne. Niuserra is included on Manetho's king list as _Rathures_, which is the Greek transcription of his name.
