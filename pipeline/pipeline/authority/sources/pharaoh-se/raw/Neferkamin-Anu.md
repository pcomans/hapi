# Neferkamin Anu in hieroglyphs

The thirteenth pharaoh of the [Eighth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/8),

| Precedessor<br>[Neferkara Pepiseneb](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Neferkara-Pepiseneb)<br>Successor<br>[Qakara Ibi](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Qakara-Ibi) |
| Reign of **Neferkamin Anu** |

The name Seneferka Anu is found on the Abydos king list number 52, which translates to the odd _the one whom a ka has made perfect, Anu_. The sign emblem for the god Min, R22 (mn) and O34 (s) are very similar, and a small mistake by a scribe confusing "Min" with "S" is easily understood. Exchanging the signs makes the name Neferkamin Anu, which gives the more fitting translation of _the perfect one of the ka of Min, Anu._ The corresponding name in the Turin papyrus contains only the name _Nefer_.
