## The Royal Titulary

From the hieroglyphic records

## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Heqa kheper Ra, <i>setep en Ra</i>](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/22-03-01.svg)

Heqa kheper Ra, _setep en Ra_

ḥḳꜢ-ḫpr-rꜤ stp-n-rꜤ

The ruler is the (very) manifestation of Ra, chosen by Ra

ra-HqA-xpr-stp&n&ra

Montet, _La Nécropole royale de Tanis_ (1951), Vol. 2, 39, fig. 12

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 186-187, 4:T

## Birth name

![Nomen or birth name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_sara.svg)

Personal name given at birth. Also known as the _Nomen_.

Birth name

Shoshenq _mery Amun_

šš(nḳ) mrꞽ-ꞽmn

Shoshenq, beloved of Amun

i-mn:n:N36-M8:M8

Montet, _La Nécropole royale de Tanis_ (1951), Vol. 2, 39, fig. 12
