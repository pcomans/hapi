# Smendes I in hieroglyphs

The first pharaoh of the [Twenty-first Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/21), _a.k.a. Nesbanebdjed, Nesybanebdjedet_

| Precedessor<br>[Ramesses XI](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Ramesses-XI)<br>Successor<br>[Amenemnesut](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Amenemnesut) |
| Reign of **Smendes I** |
| AE Chronology | 1076–1052 |
| von Beckerath | 1070–1044 |
| Shaw | 1069–1043 |
| Dodson | 1064–1038 |
| Redford | 1081–1055 |
| Manetho | 26 years |

After burying Ramesses XI, he succeeded him, but only in Lower Egypt, a territory which he controlled. The High Priests of Amun at Thebes effectively controlled Upper Egypt. His Egyptian name was actually _Nisubanebdjed_, but it was transcribed into ancient Greek as _Smendes_.

## Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_horus.svg)

The oldest form of the name of a pharaoh, a falcon representing Horus is perched on the palace facade.

Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/21-01-01.svg)

Ka nakht mery Ra seuser Amun khepeshef er seqa Maat

kꜢ-nḫt-mrꞽ-rꜤ swsr-ꞽmn ḫpš.f-r-sḳꜢꞽ-mꜢꜤt

Strong bull, beloved of Ra, whose arm Amun has strengthened in order to offer up Maat

E2:D40-ra:Z1-mr-s-wsr-s-r:D40-i-mn:n-xpS:f:r-s-q-A28-U5:a-t:H8

RecTrav 10, 135 (1)

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 178-179, 1:H

## Nebty name

![Nebty name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nebty.svg)

## Throne name

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Hedj kheper Ra, <i>setep en Ra</i>](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/21-01-04.svg)

Hedj kheper Ra, _setep en Ra_

ḥḏ-ḫpr-rꜤ stp.n-rꜤ

The dazzling one is the (very) manifestation of Ra

ra-S1-xpr-stp&n&ra

RecTrav 10, 135 (2)

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 178-179, 1:T
