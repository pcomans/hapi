## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Aa kheperu Ra, <i>setep en Amun</i>](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/22-12-01.svg)

Aa kheperu Ra, _setep en Amun_

ꜤꜢ-ḫprw-rꜤ stp.n-ꞽmn

The great one is a manifestation of Ra, chosen by Amun

ra:aA-xpr-C12-stp:n

Posener, _Mélanges Gamal eddin Mokhtar_ (1985), II, 266

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 200-201, 2:T1

Throne name variant

![<i>Nisu-bity</i> Aa kheper Ra, <i>setep en Amun</i>](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/22-12-02.svg)

_Nisu-bity_ Aa kheper Ra, _setep en Amun_

nꞽ-swt-bꞽt ꜤꜢ-ḫpr-rꜤ stp.n-ꞽmn

(The Dual King) The great one is a manifestation of Ra, chosen by Amun

N6B-ra:aA-xpr-C12-stp:n

Posener, _Mélanges Gamal eddin Mokhtar_ (1985), II, 264

Beckerath, “Handbuch der ägyptischen Königsnamen”, 2nd ed. _MÄS_ **49** (1999). 200-201, 2:T2
