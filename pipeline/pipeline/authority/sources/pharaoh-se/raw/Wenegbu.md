# Wenegbu in hieroglyphs

The sixth pharaoh of the [Predynastic kings](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/predynastic), _a.k.a. Wazner, Wadjenedj_

| Precedessor<br>[Neheb](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Neheb)<br>Successor<br>[Mekh](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Mekh) |
| Reign of **Wenegbu** |
|  | 4th millenium BCE |

**Wenegbu** is found on the Palermo Stone as ruler of a predynastic Lower Egypt. As this is the only known record of the name, it is impossible to know whether he was a real or mythical.
