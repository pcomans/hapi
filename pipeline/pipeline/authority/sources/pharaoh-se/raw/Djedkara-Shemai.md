# Djedkara Shemai in hieroglyphs

The fifth pharaoh of the [Eighth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/8), _a.k.a. Djedkare Shemay_

| Precedessor<br>[Neferkara Neby](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Neferkara-Neby)<br>Successor<br>[Neferkara Khendu](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Neferkara-Khendu) |
| Reign of **Djedkara Shemai** |

Djedkara Shemai is found on the Abydos king list number 44, while the Turin papyrus has a notation of a lacuna where the name would have been found.
