# Seka in hieroglyphs

The first pharaoh of the [Predynastic kings](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/predynastic), _a.k.a. Hsekiu_

**Seka** is found on the Palermo Stone as ruler of a predynastic Lower Egypt. As this is the only known record of the name, it is impossible to know whether he was a real or mythical.
