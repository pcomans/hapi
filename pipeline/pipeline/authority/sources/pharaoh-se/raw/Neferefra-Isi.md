# Neferefra Isi in hieroglyphs

The fifth pharaoh of the [Fifth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/5), _a.k.a. Neferefre, Raneferef_

Neferefra was the eldest son of Neferirkara, but he died after only about a year of rule. It is possible that Shepseskara managed to usurp the throne for a short period before being ousted by Neferefra's brother Niuserra. He is included on Manetho's king list as _Kheres_, which is a greek transcription of the erroneous name _Khaneferra_ from the records of the Ramesside scribes.

## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.
