The name of the god Thoth is an ancient Greek transcription of the original name _Djehuty_. Thutmose is a modern compromise between the Greek transcription _Thutmosis_ and _Djehutimes_. Known as _Khebron_ or _Khebros_ by Manetho, he was placed as the successor of Ahmose, though he was in fact not the second king of the 18th dynasty, but the fourth. The reign order of Manetho is quite unreliable for the entire 18th dynasty. He married his half-sister, Hatshepsut, that would soon become pharaoh.
