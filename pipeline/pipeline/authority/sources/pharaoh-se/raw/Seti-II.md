# Seti II in hieroglyphs

The fifth pharaoh of the [Nineteenth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/19), _a.k.a. Sethos II_

Seti II was the son of Merenptah and a grandson of the great Ramesses II. He succeeded his father on the throne, but his reign was complicated by a rival king, _Amenmesse_, who seized power in Upper Egypt and Nubia sometime between years 2 and 4 of Seti II's reign. This usurper was eventually dealt with.
