Amenhotep IV reigned for five years before changing his name to **Akhenaten**, keeping only the throne name of his original titulary. He is especially famed for introducing the worship of Aten above all other gods, sometimes described as monotheism, which it was not. His name in the Amarna letters is _Nibhururiya_, which is the Akkadian renditon of his throne name Neferkheperura.
