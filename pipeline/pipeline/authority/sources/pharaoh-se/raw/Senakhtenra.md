# Senakhtenra in hieroglyphs

The seventh pharaoh of the [Seventeenth Dynasty](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/17), _a.k.a. Senakhtenre Tao in older texts_

| Precedessor<br>[Intef VII](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Intef-VII)<br>Successor<br>[Seqenenra](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Seqenenra) |
| Reign of **Senakhtenra** |
| Ryholt | 1559–1558 |

For a long time there were no contemporary attestations of this pharaoh, so it was proposed that his birth name was Tao, like his son, as two pharaohs with that name are mentioned in the Abbott papyrus. This was disproved in 2012 when his Horus and birth names were discovered at Karnak.

## Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_horus.svg)

The oldest form of the name of a pharaoh, a falcon representing Horus is perched on the palace facade.

Horus name

![Horus name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/17-07-01.svg)

mrꞽ-mꜢꜤt

## Throne names

![Prenomen or throne name](https://pharaoh.se/svg/hiero/t_nesut.svg)

Announced at the coronation and always written in a cartouche. The “official” name of the pharaoh. Also known as the _Prenomen_.

Throne name

![Senakht en Ra](https://pharaoh.se/svg/pharaoh/17-07-02.svg)

Senakht en Ra

snḫt.n-rꜤ

The one whom Ra has made victorious

ra-z:xt:x\*t-D40:n

Karnak Canon no. 26
