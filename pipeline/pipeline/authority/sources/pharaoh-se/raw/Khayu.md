# Khayu in hieroglyphs

The second pharaoh of the [Predynastic kings](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/dynasty/predynastic), _a.k.a. Iukha_

| Precedessor<br>[Seka](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Seka)<br>Successor<br>[Tiu](https://pharaoh.se/ancient-egypt/pharaoh/Tiu) |
| Reign of **Khayu** |
|  | 4th millenium BCE |

**Khayu** is found on the Palermo Stone as ruler of a predynastic Lower Egypt. As this is the only known record of the name, it is impossible to know whether he was a real or mythical.
